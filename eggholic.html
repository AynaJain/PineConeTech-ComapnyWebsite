EGG-O-HOLIC

Eggholic’s mission is to bring authentic Indian street food to America – to delight their customer’s palates with exciting flavors. Our challenge was to build a brand identity that perfectly represents their vision and flavors. The services included logo creation, website development, menu design (digital displays, restaurant take-out and dine in). In addition, we also developed their product photography and social media marketing on Facebook and Instagram. Pinecone is also developing Eggholic’s new franchising structure, which will help every location stay on brand.
