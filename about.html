Why Pinecone? As a symbol, the pinecone is known to represent the pineal gland, human enlightenment, and the third eye. Pinecones grow on one of the oldest plant species on Earth. They are neither fruits nor seeds but carry developing seeds inside. Like our namesake, we at Pinecone are seasoned professionals carrying imaginative solutions to fruition.

Pinecone Tech is a collective of creative professionals bringing innovative solutions to brands and organizations across the private and public sector. With a combined 26 years of experience providing creative business, design, and tech solutions, our team takes a passionate approach to solving the technical and marketing needs of our clients.

Whether you are looking to enhance your brand, broaden your reach, or discover technical software or application solutions for your business, we are committed to delivering creative solutions and fresh perspectives with a practical approach.
